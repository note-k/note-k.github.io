Partners

We work with partner companies, leaders in their sector, to provide added value to our customers.

GFT​

GFT is a digital transformation pioneer developing sustainable solutions based on new technologies, including artificial intelligence and blockchain/DLT. GFT experts create and implement scalable software applications that make access to innovative business models secure and easy.

In addition to being our technological point of reference for the development and evolution of the FrameK platform and its integrations, guaranteeing us the highest quality and safety standards, GFT is also a worldwide commercial partner for the offer of services based on utility tokens.
